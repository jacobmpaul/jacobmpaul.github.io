Never odd or even

Dr Jacob M. Paul

"What is a number that a brain my know it, and a brain that it may know a number?" adapted from McCulloch (1960).


My name is Jacob, I am a cognitive neuroscientist interested in how we interact with a quantiative world of numbers and mathematics.

I'm currently a postdoctoral researcher in the School of Psychological Sciences at the University of Melbourne, Australia.

Previously I worked as a postdoc with Prof Ben Harvey in the Department of Experimental Psychology at Utrecht University, Netherlands.
